How to take a screenshot.

On Mac

When on mac there are a series of key shortcuts to complete various commands.

  • Entire Screen: CMD + Shift + 3.
  • Selected Portion: CMD + Shift + 4 then drag your crosshair over the area you want screenshotted.
  • Single Window: CMD + Shift + 4 + Spacebar then click on your desired window.
  • Screenshot Menu: CMD + Shift + 5 for screen recording and options.
